Data description:
-----------------------------------------

hc_gmv.mat:  
healthy control gray matter volumns, regional GMV lists based on HCP360 Atlas;

mdd_gmv.mat: 
MDD patients gray matter volumns, regional GMV lists based on HCP360 Atlas;

age_sex_edu_data.mat: 
information of age(years), gender(1/2), education(years) of HC and MDD;

tiv_data.mat: 
whole brain volumns of all participants;

HCP360_regions_name.mat: 
regions name of HCP360 Atlas; 

medication_effects.mat:
medication informations(0/1);

------------------------------------------
gmv_all_r.mat:
regressed regional GMV of HC and MDD;
significantly abnormal regions index;

gmv_id.mat:
patients and controls sample ids;

methy_index.mat:
methylation data sample ids;

sig_methy_cpg_2.mat:
individual DNA methylation beta values of DMPs of HC and MDD patients;

demethy_pls_genes05.mat:
overlapped genes of DMPs genes and PLS genes;

cpg_site.mat:
DMP cpg sites；

pathway_gene_targetpath.mat：
genes significantly enriched in the top biological processing pathways;
